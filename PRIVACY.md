# Privacy Policy

**Screeps SC does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

The extension runs entirely in your browser. It contains no analytics, tracking,
or telemetry, and it never sends any data to the developer or to any third party.

The only credential it touches is your existing screeps.com session token, which
it reads from screeps.com's own storage solely to make requests back to the
screeps.com API on your behalf (for actions you initiate, such as market deals).
That token is only ever sent to screeps.com — never to the developer or anyone
else — and is not stored by the extension.
